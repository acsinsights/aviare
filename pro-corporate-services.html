Complete Range of PRO Services

Our PRO services cover every administrative and regulatory need your business may encounter in the UAE. Each task is handled directly by our trained PRO team with constant status updates through your account manager.

Employment & Investor Visa Management
  • End-to-end support for new employment visas, investor visas, and dependent visas.
  • Coordination for medical tests, Emirates ID applications, and visa stamping.
  • Renewal of employment and investor visas before expiry to avoid fines.
  • Visa cancellations and transfers between companies or sponsors.
  • Quota applications and modifications to accommodate business growth.
  • Absconding submission and removal services when required under MOHRE guidelines.
Company Documentation & Licensing
  • Trade license renewals and amendments (activities, shareholders, or name changes).
  • DED services including activity approvals, new license registration, and modifications.
  • Chamber of Commerce certification and renewals.
  • Company Establishment Card renewals and immigration card updates.
  • PO Box renewals and stamp ordering for company documentation.
  • New E-signature card issuance and labour card renewals/updates.
  • Bank guarantee refunds and coordination with authorities.
Legal & Attestation Services
  • MOFA attestations for corporate and personal documents.
  • Legal translations for Arabic-English documentation.
  • Good Conduct Certificates and lost document reporting.
  • Health insurance processing for employees as per UAE labour law.
  • Power of Attorney preparation and notarization through approved legal partners.
🧑 Labour & Administrative Support
  • Drafting and submission of offer letters and labour contracts.
  • Handling relative and temporary work permits.
  • Typing and processing of medical applications and labour cards.
  • Approvals for new license registration with the relevant authorities.
  • Weekly visa and process updates shared directly with your HR or management.
