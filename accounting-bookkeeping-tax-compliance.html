Accounting, Bookkeeping & Tax Compliance in the UAE

Accurate, Transparent & Technology-Driven Financial Management for Growing Businesses

Every successful business depends on one thing — clear, reliable financial data. At Aviare Business Management, we provide full-spectrum accounting, bookkeeping, and tax compliance services that give business owners the clarity they need to make confident decisions, stay compliant, and grow sustainably.

Our system is designed around accuracy, accessibility, and accountability — supported by technology and delivered by a dedicated accounting team that knows your business inside out.

Why Accounting & Compliance Matter in the UAE

The UAE's financial landscape has evolved rapidly. With the introduction of Corporate Tax, VAT, and digital reporting mandates, companies are now required to maintain proper accounting records and submit accurate filings.

Failure to comply can lead to heavy fines, penalties, or even license suspensions.

Our approach goes beyond basic bookkeeping — we act as your financial partner, ensuring every transaction, return, and statement is prepared and filed correctly, on time, every time.

What's Included in Our Accounting & Tax Compliance Services

Monthly Bookkeeping (on Accrual Basis)
  • Professional bookkeeping on an accrual basis for accurate profit/loss reflection.
  • Monthly maintenance of books for up to 10 transactions per plan (expandable as you grow).
  • Paperless document storage — we digitize and organize all your invoices, receipts, and vouchers.
  • Monthly review of books by your dedicated bookkeeper to ensure every entry is validated.
  • Access to an interactive dashboard showing insights, charts, and recommendations.

"With Aviare, you'll always know where your money is going — in real time."

Technology Integration for Ease & Transparency
  • Mobile App for Invoicing & Expense Upload: Snap and upload your bills directly — our system syncs everything.
  • Dashboard Access: Get monthly insights on revenue, expenses, profitability, and cash flow.
  • Paperless Operations: No files, no physical ledgers — just organized digital storage that's accessible anytime.

Your accounting data stays secure, confidential, and accessible to you 24/7 through our CRM-based financial system.

VAT Services
  • VAT Registration (for eligible businesses under FTA rules).
  • VAT Return Preparation & Filing every quarter or as per schedule.
  • VAT Review & Reconciliation to verify accuracy before filing.
  • VAT Advisory Services to ensure correct tax treatment of sales and purchases.
  • Voluntary or Deregistration when your business outgrows or no longer requires VAT registration.

We ensure that every filing is FTA-compliant and supported with proper documentation in case of an audit.

Corporate Tax Services
  • Corporate Tax Registration under UAE's Ministry of Finance.
  • Corporate Tax Return Preparation & Filing in line with the latest regulations.
  • Tax Planning & Advisory Services — unlimited consultation on optimizing structure, deductions, and compliance.
  • Preparation of Financial Reports and tax computation sheets for audit support.

We don't just calculate — we strategize, ensuring your company pays what's fair while maintaining a clean record with authorities.

Financial Reporting & Review
  • Accurate Financial Statements — balance sheet, profit & loss, and cash flow.
  • Monthly and Quarterly Reports customized for management review.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Our team highlights cost trends, profit margins, and potential savings.
  • Auditor Coordination: Assistance during statutory audits or financial reviews.

You'll have the kind of clarity that makes decision-making easy — not overwhelming.
