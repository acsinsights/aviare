Visa Process Services in the UAE

Seamless Visa, Immigration & Compliance Management for Your Business

Setting up or running a business in the UAE goes far beyond obtaining your trade licence.

Every company must navigate a complex visa and immigration system — issuing investor visas, managing labour cards, renewing Emirates IDs, and cancelling or transferring sponsorships when needed.

Missing even one deadline can lead to fines, visa holds, or operational disruptions.

That's why Aviare Business Management provides a complete visa lifecycle management service — handling every step, every renewal, and every compliance task, so you can stay focused on growing your business, not chasing paperwork.

Why Visa Process Services Matter

Visa and immigration management in the UAE involves coordination with multiple authorities — the Ministry of Human Resources & Emiratisation (MOHRE), Immigration, and the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs & Port Security (ICP).

While anyone can submit a form, the difference lies in understanding the sequence, timing, and compliance of each stage.

Our team's deep knowledge of UAE regulations and experience across mainland and free zone jurisdictions ensures your processes are always handled accurately and on time.

Without expert guidance, even simple mistakes — like missing a medical test or submitting an outdated document — can cause major delays. With Aviare, that risk disappears.

Our Visa Process Services

We provide end-to-end visa and immigration solutions tailored for entrepreneurs, corporations, and global investors.

Employee, Partner & Investor Visas
  • Complete management of work, investor, and partner visas.
  • Handling of labour permit, entry permit, medical test, Emirates ID, and visa stamping.
  • Visa quota planning and eligibility monitoring for new hires and expansions.
Renewals, Cancellations & Transfers
  • Timely renewals of residence visas and labour cards before expiry.
  • Visa cancellations when employees resign or transfer.
  • Sponsorship transfers for business expansion or restructuring.
  • Compliance monitoring to prevent penalties and blacklisting.
Dependent & Family Sponsorship
  • Assistance in sponsoring spouse, children, or parents.
  • Guidance on income and accommodation eligibility.
  • Complete documentation, medical, and ID processing.
Documentation & Government Liaison
  • Coordination with MOHRE, Immigration, and ICP.
  • Document attestation, translation, notarisation, and submission.
  • Status updates and follow-ups through our digital CRM dashboard.
Visa Tracking & Compliance Alerts
  • Real-time tracking of all active visas under your company.
  • Automated reminders for renewals and medical revalidations.
  • Regular compliance reports and risk alerts.
