Bank Account Assistance in the UAE

Secure Your Financial Foundation – Without The Hassle

Opening a corporate bank account is one of the most essential and time-sensitive steps when starting or scaling your business in the UAE.

Yet for many entrepreneurs, the process turns out to be more complicated than expected — multiple document requests, compliance checks, and long follow-ups with bank officers.

At Aviare Business Management, we simplify that journey.

We help you choose the right banking partner, prepare every document, and coordinate directly with banks to ensure your corporate account opens quickly, smoothly, and without unnecessary stress.

Why Bank Account Setup Matters More Than You Think

Opening a business account in the UAE is not a one-form process — it's a strategic step that determines how efficiently you'll operate and how confidently partners and clients will transact with you.

Here's what most businesses don't realise until it's too late:

Banking criteria vary across free zones, mainland entities, and business activities.

Each bank has unique requirements for documents, business models, and compliance checks.

Delays can impact your operations — without an active bank account, you can't process payments, pay suppliers, or receive client funds.

Your corporate bank account signals reliability, transparency, and readiness for growth. Doing it wrong can delay onboarding, vendor contracts, or even your visa processes.

That's where Aviare steps in to make things easy and predictable.

Our Bank Account Assistance Services

We don't just tell you what to submit — we walk you through every step and manage the process end-to-end.

Bank Selection & Advisory
  • Identify the most suitable bank based on your licence type, company structure, and transaction needs.
  • Compare UAE local and international banks for your industry requirements.
  • Evaluate account options (current, savings, or multi-currency) aligned with your business goals.
Documentation & Compliance Preparation
  • Prepare all required documents: Memorandum of Association, trade licence, tenancy contract, proof of address, and business plan.
  • Assist with KYC and due diligence forms to ensure fast approvals.
  • Organize shareholder and management documentation to meet each bank's compliance checklist.
End-to-End Coordination with Banks
  • Submit applications and liaise directly with bank representatives on your behalf.
  • Schedule and manage any required meetings or interviews.
  • Handle clarifications, follow-ups, and escalation support if any issues arise.
Account Activation & Post-Approval Setup
  • Support with online banking activation, cheque book issuance, and debit/credit card setup.
  • Link your account to your accounting or invoicing system for smoother cash flow.
  • Set up payment workflows and vendor payment templates for efficiency.
Multi-Currency & International Banking Options
  • Assistance with multi-currency accounts (AED, USD, EUR, GBP, etc.).
  • Setup of international transfer facilities and trade finance services.
  • Guidance on banking for cross-border or holding companies.
