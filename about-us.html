Who We Are

Get Consulting For Better Business Growth

Aviare Business Management is a Dubai-based professional services firm dedicated to helping entrepreneurs, SMEs, and multinational investors move into and grow within the UAE market. Backed by deep local expertise and a client-first approach, Aviare provides complete company formation, PRO and government liaison, HR & payroll, bookkeeping, VAT and corporate tax compliance, and strategic business advisory services — all under one roof.
Our mission is simple: to remove friction from your UAE business journey so you can focus on growth. We combine market intelligence, hands-on operational support, and modern technology to deliver fast, compliant, and cost-efficient outcomes for every client.
