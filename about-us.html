What We Offer

Our Core Services

Company Formation

Mainland, Free Zone and Offshore entity setup with tailored licensing, shareholder structuring and bank account support.

We match the legal jurisdiction to your activity and market goals.

PRO & Government Liaison

End-to-end handling of visas, Emirates IDs, labour approvals, and all government interactions so your operations stay compliant and uninterrupted.

HR & Payroll (WPS)

Full payroll processing and WPS-compliant salary distribution; we ensure your employee payments meet UAE regulatory requirements.

The UAE requires wages to be paid through approved electronic channels under the Wage Protection System to ensure compliance and worker protection.

Bookkeeping & Tax Compliance

Monthly bookkeeping, VAT registration and filing, corporate tax registration and ongoing advisory.

UAE VAT is applied at a standard rate of 5% and businesses must register if supplies exceed the statutory threshold.

Corporate Tax Advisory

We guide clients through the UAE federal corporate tax landscape, including registration and planning for the regime that applies to financial years beginning on or after June 1, 2023; profits above the AED 375,000 threshold are generally subject to a 9% rate (with special rules for very large multinationals).
