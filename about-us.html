Who We Are

Aviare Business Management is a Dubai-based professional services firm dedicated to helping entrepreneurs, SMEs, and multinational investors move into and grow within the UAE market. Backed by deep local expertise and a client-first approach, Aviare provides complete company formation, PRO and government liaison, HR & payroll, bookkeeping, VAT and corporate tax compliance, and strategic business advisory services — all under one roof.

Our mission is simple: to remove friction from your UAE business journey so you can focus on growth. We combine market intelligence, hands-on operational support, and modern technology to deliver fast, compliant, and cost-efficient outcomes for every client.

Our Story & Experience

Empowering Growth in the UAE

Founded to answer a common challenge — how to enter the UAE quickly and compliantly — Aviare has grown into a trusted partner for businesses across industries. Our team's market intelligence spans the full lifecycle of business set-up and operations in the UAE, and our consultants bring years of sector-specific know-how to every engagement.

We proudly serve startups, scale-ups, regional hubs, and global parents seeking to establish local presence. From a single-activity free zone entity to complex mainland or offshore structures, Aviare's approach is practical, transparent, and results-driven.

Trust & Governance

Our Commitment to Compliance & Security

Aviare places a premium on transparency, data security, and regulatory compliance. All client records are stored in a secure CRM system, and our processes are continuously updated to reflect the latest legal and tax clarifications issued by UAE authorities.
