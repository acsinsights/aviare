Visa Process & PRO Services in the UAE

Seamless Immigration & Government Liaison for Your Business

Setting up or running a business in the UAE isn't just about getting a licence. Behind the scenes, there are multiple steps: visas to issue, labour cards to register, Emirates IDs to track, renewals to schedule. Miss one, and your company could face fines. Miss two, and operations might stall.

At Aviare Business Management, we don't just handle the main application — we manage the entire lifelong compliance loop so you can focus on your business, not the paperwork.

Why Visa & PRO Services Matter

Anyone can fill out a form, but the difference lies in knowing the "when" and "how" of the process: which department waits longest, which document needs notarising, and where potential delays crop up. That's the value of PRO (Public Relations Officer) services.

Outsourced PRO teams help businesses with visas, labour approvals, document attestations, and liaison with government authorities. Without expert support, businesses often end up chasing visits, missing deadlines or repeating processes.

Our PRO & Visa Support Services

We offer full-spectrum support — from the first application to every renewal and cancellation. Here are the major services we deliver:

Employee & Investor Visas
  • We help sponsor visas for employees, consultants, investors and their dependents.
  • We handle the labour permit/entry permit process, Emirates ID registration, medical test, visa stamping and residence card.
  • We monitor quota rules, age limits and any changes to visa eligibility.
Renewals, Cancellations & Transfers
  • Renewal of residence visas and labour cards before expiration.
  • Cancellation of visas and company sponsorship when employees leave.
  • Transfers of sponsorship between companies when your business grows or restructures.
  • Ensuring that you stay compliant and avoid fines.
Documentation & Government Liaison
  • We act as the bridge between your company and ministries (labour, immigration, MOHRE, ICP etc.).
  • We manage attestation, translations, document submissions, approvals and follow-ups.
  • We keep you updated so you're never left uncertain about what's next.
Strategic Planning & Compliance Monitoring
  • We build a visa roadmap aligned with your business growth plan — e.g., when you'll hire more staff, need new visas, or expand into new Emirates.
  • We provide notifications, track renewal dates, ensure nothing is overlooked.
  • Compliance isn't an annual task — it's continuous. We help you embed that.
