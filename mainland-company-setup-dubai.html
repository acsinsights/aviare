Licenses
1

Professional License in Dubai, UAE

This license is for service-oriented endeavors and professionals. The cost is lower compared to other licenses, making it an ideal choice for consultants, freelancers, and service providers looking to establish their presence in Dubai.

View Details
2

Commercial License in Dubai, UAE

This license is for buying and selling goods or services (trading). It is common in Dubai and UAE mainland, and formalities are easily accomplished. Perfect for businesses engaged in import/export, wholesale, retail, and distribution activities.

View Details
3

Industrial License in Dubai, UAE

License issued for firms that are involved in the manufacture of goods like factories, industries, etc. When it comes to an industrial license, you may need to seek the approval of external ministries and government departments, depending on the type of industrial activity.

View Details
4

Tourism License in Dubai, UAE

All tourism-related activities require a valid tourism license. The Department of Tourism and Commerce Marketing (DTCM) has a major role in the issuance of tourism licenses. A valid tourism license is mandatory to conduct inbound and outbound tour-related activities.

View Details
5

E-Trader License in Dubai, UAE

Explore Dubai's digital sector with an E-trader license.
