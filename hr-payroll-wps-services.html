HR, Payroll & WPS Services in the UAE

Streamlined, Compliant, and Secure Payroll Management for Every Business

Managing payroll and HR compliance in the UAE can quickly become complex — from keeping up with WPS regulations to ensuring accurate salary payments and visa-linked data updates.

At Aviare Business Management, we take that responsibility off your shoulders. Our HR and Payroll division is built around three pillars: accuracy, compliance, and confidentiality.

Whether you're a growing SME or a large enterprise, our dedicated HR and payroll experts make sure your team gets paid on time, every time — while keeping your business fully compliant with UAE labour laws.

Our Complete HR & Payroll Services

Payroll Processing
  • Salary calculation and disbursement as per UAE Labour Law.
  • Monthly processing for all employees, including bonuses, deductions, and overtime.
  • Automatic payroll reports for management review.
  • Salary slips and payment confirmations generated through the system.
  • Integration with your accounting module or dashboard for real-time financial tracking.
Wage Protection System (WPS) Services
  • Company Registration and Activation under the UAE Wage Protection System.
  • Fast and Secure Salary Transfers via approved exchange houses or banks.
  • Compliance Statements confirming that wages and salaries have been paid on time.
  • Monthly WPS File Preparation & Submission as per UAE Central Bank format.
  • WPS Monitoring & Alerts — we alert you of any discrepancies before penalties occur.
  • Update WPS in Case of Employee Cancellation or end of service.

“Our WPS team acts as an extension of your HR department — ensuring 100% compliance without the administrative burden.”

Employee Lifecycle Support

We handle every major step of employee management to ensure smooth HR operations:

  • Offer letter drafting and employee onboarding.
  • Labour card application, renewal, and update.
  • Medical insurance coordination.
  • Emirates ID registration and renewal.
  • End-of-service settlements and gratuity calculations.
  • Absconding submission and removal when required.

Every stage of your employee journey — from joining to final settlement — is documented, transparent, and compliant.

HR Administration & Compliance
  • MOHRE and immigration compliance monitoring.
  • Monthly HR compliance reporting.
  • Document attestation and renewal reminders.
  • Employee record maintenance through secure cloud storage.
  • Regular audits to ensure adherence to UAE labour law.

We don’t just execute tasks — we continuously audit and improve your HR compliance process so you never face legal or administrative issues.
