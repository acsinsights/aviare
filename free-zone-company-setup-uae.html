Free Zone Company Setup in the UAE

Build Your Global Business with 100% Ownership, Speed, and Simplicity

If you’re looking to start a business in the UAE but want complete ownership, minimal bureaucracy, and cost-efficient setup — a Free Zone license may be exactly what you need.

At Aviare Business Management, we’ve helped hundreds of entrepreneurs, investors, and global startups establish their companies in the UAE’s most successful Free Zones — from Dubai Multi Commodities Centre (DMCC) and IFZA, to RAKEZ, Sharjah Media City, and more.

We take the guesswork out of the process and make your business launch in the UAE a smooth experience — with full transparency, clarity, and support at every step.

Why Choose a Free Zone Setup?

Free Zones were created to make the UAE a global hub for trade and innovation. They’re designed for entrepreneurs who want speed, ownership, and global reach without heavy red tape.

100% Foreign Ownership

No need for a local partner or sponsor.

Full Profit Repatriation

Move funds freely to and from your home country.

Tax Advantages

Zero import/export duties and corporate tax exemptions (zone dependent).

Quick Licensing

Approvals often completed in just 3–5 working days.

Strategic Locations

Choose hubs like Business Bay, JLT, Sharjah, or Ras Al Khaimah.

Modern Infrastructure

Premium offices, logistics, ports, and airports nearby.

How Aviare Simplifies the Free Zone Setup

Our job is to help you find the best Free Zone for your specific business activity, budget, and visa requirements. There are more than 40 Free Zones across the UAE — each with its own rules, costs, and specialties. We do the heavy lifting for you.

Step 1: Free Zone Selection

We start by understanding your business model and shortlist Free Zones that align best.

  • Type of license required (service, trading, industrial, holding)
  • Number of visas you’ll need
  • Whether you need a physical office or a flexi-desk
  • Renewal costs and expansion flexibility
Step 2: Company Name & Initial Approval

We check trade name availability and handle all initial approvals with the authority.

Step 3: Document Submission

We prepare, translate, and submit all necessary documents to the respective Free Zone.

Step 4: Lease & License Issuance
  • Flexi Desk – shared workstation
  • Dedicated Desk – reserved space
  • Private Office – enclosed, branded space

Once the lease is in place, the Free Zone issues your trade license.

Step 5: Visa & Bank Account Setup

We handle investor and employee visas, Emirates ID, medical tests, and bank account opening support.

Popular Free Zones We Work With

  • Dubai: DMCC, IFZA, DSOA, Dubai South, Meydan, Dubai Media City
  • Sharjah: Shams, SPC Free Zone
  • Ras Al Khaimah: RAKEZ
  • Fujairah & Ajman: Ajman Free Zone, Fujairah Creative City
  • Abu Dhabi: ADGM, twofour54

Each Free Zone has its own advantages — our consultants will walk you through comparisons to find your ideal match.
