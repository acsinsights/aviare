Bank Account Assistance & WPS Services

Secure Your Financial Foundation in the UAE – Without The Headaches

If you're establishing or scaling a business in the UAE, one of the earliest and most critical steps is securing the right bank account and setting up your Wage Protection System (WPS) processes. Many company-owners don't realise how much time, documentation and follow-up this demands until they're in the middle of it and under pressure.

At Aviare Business Management, we help you navigate both these essential pieces — from choosing a banking partner that suits your industry and structure, to ensuring your salary payments comply with UAE regulations, smoothly and proactively.

Why Bank Account & WPS Setup Matter More Than You Think

Opening a corporate bank account in the UAE isn't just "fill in an application and you're done." There are major considerations:

Banking criteria differ by free-zone vs mainland, by the nature of your business and by regulator rules.

Many banks require proof of activity, clients, business model, document originals, and you must be ready.

Once you hire staff, you must pay them through the WPS — the UAE's official system that ensures salaries are timely and traceable. Mistakes can lead to fines, delays in visa issuance, or even trouble with your licence.

Your financial setup sends a signal: reliable, compliant, ready for growth — or the opposite.

If you try to do it all yourself, you'll likely spend weeks chasing bank approvals, waiting on visa stamping, or scrambling to find documents when you realise you don't have them. That's lost time and lost focus on your actual business.

That's where Aviare steps in.

Our Bank Account & WPS Support Services

We walk alongside you for every step — we don't hand you a checklist and say "good luck."

Bank Account Assistance
  • We help you select the right bank (UAE or international bank with UAE presence), and understand which account type fits your business licence, industry and structure.
  • We prepare all required documentation: board resolutions, MOA/AOA, proof of lease, business plan, bank reference letters, identity checks.
  • We coordinate with the bank on your behalf — submitting the application, following up, arranging meetings or interviews if needed.
  • We ensure you understand the terms: minimum balances, service fees, transaction limits, multi-currency options, and online banking features.
  • Post-approval support: setting up online access, card issuance, linking to your accounting system, setting up payables/receivables flow.
WPS & Salary Disbursement Services
  • We set up your company under the UAE Wage Protection System (WPS) so that your staff salaries comply with MOHRE regulations.
  • Monthly payroll review: checking salary amounts, transfer dates, bank crediting, adjusting for bonus/overtime/deductions.
  • Salary file preparation and submission to bank & regulatory channels.
  • Visa-linked salary compliance: ensuring that every resident's salary is processed correctly so that visa renewals are not blocked.
  • Ongoing monitoring: we alert you ahead of any upcoming renewal, audit or compliance change.
