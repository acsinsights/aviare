Find Your Perfect Office Space in Dubai

Flexible, Affordable & Fully Equipped Workspaces Tailored for Your Business

If you’ve been searching for an office in Dubai, you already know the challenge — too many listings, too many agents, and no clear way to know what truly fits your business. At Aviare Business Management, we simplify that entire process.

Through our network of 100+ verified office spaces across Dubai, we help you find the ideal workspace that matches your budget, team size, and growth plan. Whether you’re setting up a startup, opening a branch, or simply need a professional business address for licensing, we’ll connect you with the right solution — quickly, transparently, and cost-effectively.

Choose the Workspace That Fits You

We understand that every business operates differently. That’s why we offer every type of office option available in Dubai’s top business districts:

Private Offices

For teams that need privacy, control, and a professional environment.

  • Fully furnished spaces
  • 24/7 access and dedicated meeting rooms
  • High-speed internet, utilities, and maintenance included
  • Suitable for trade license registration

Perfect for established businesses and entrepreneurs who want an immediate operational setup.

Dedicated Desks

Ideal for small teams or independent professionals.

  • Fixed desk in a shared workspace
  • Access to conference rooms and business lounges
  • Great for networking and cost efficiency
  • Approved for visa and licensing requirements

You get all the structure of an office without paying for unnecessary space.

Flexi Desks

Designed for maximum freedom.

  • Use any available desk within a co-working space
  • Access during business hours
  • Mail handling, reception, and support services included
  • Cost-effective solution for freelancers or early-stage startups

If your work is mobile, this is your most flexible option — work where and when you want, with zero overhead.

Virtual Offices

Professional address. Real credibility.

  • Use a verified Dubai business address for your license
  • Mail and call handling included
  • Optional meeting room access
  • 100% remote — no physical space required

A perfect solution for consultants, online entrepreneurs, and global firms testing the UAE market before committing to a physical presence.

Prime Locations Across Dubai

We’ve handpicked workspaces across every major commercial district so you can choose your location strategically — close to your clients, suppliers, or talent pool. Popular business hubs include:

  • Downtown Dubai: Premium towers near Burj Khalifa and DIFC — perfect for corporate presence.
  • Business Bay: Central, modern, and ideal for startups and consultancies.
  • Jumeirah Lake Towers (JLT): Vibrant community, affordable rents, and easy metro access.
  • Al Qusais & Deira: Budget-friendly spaces with great connectivity to Sharjah and old Dubai.
  • Dubai Silicon Oasis & Media City: Tech and creative clusters with flexible co-working environments.

Whether you want prestige, affordability, or flexibility, Aviare connects you directly to the right provider — no middlemen, no hidden costs.
